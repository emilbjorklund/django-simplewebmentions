Well, hello there!

You have reached this website’s Webmention endpoint.

This is a URL that is prepared to receive POST requests telling it about content published elsewhere (or here) that mentions (by way of hyperlink) content that resides on this site.

The request should include a source-parameter, telling this site which (full) URL is mentioning it, as well as a target-parameter, containg the full URL of the piece of content on this site that it is mentioning.

You can’t really do much on this page in your browser, but it was lovely of you to stop by.
